Research

How I contribute to nutrition science

My work spans four interconnected domains — each advancing our understanding of how food shapes human health at a mechanistic level.

Clinical Trials

Coordinating a 12-week randomized crossover trial examining how dry bean market classes affect the gut microbiota and cardiometabolic health markers in humans (IRB Approved).

IRB Approved · ClinicalTrials.gov

Gut Microbiome Analysis

Performing DNA extraction, qPCR, stool pH, moisture, and short-chain fatty acid analysis to characterize microbiome shifts in response to dietary interventions.

qPCR · SCFA · Metagenomics

Systematic Reviews

Applying hybrid human-AI approaches with ASReview and TERA-Tool to screen 1,800+ citations, synthesizing evidence on dietary pulses and gut/metabolic health.

AI-Assisted · 1,800+ Citations

Precision Nutrition

Investigating pulse market classes as a framework for personalised dietary recommendations, linking nutrient profiles to individual microbiome responses and cardiometabolic outcomes.

Personalised · Microbiome-Linked
